## 1. Purpose

This document turns source research into a practical outreach queue.

It is for the sources where Signal Scout should seek permission, agreement, partnership, or at least a clear written comfort statement before building production ingestion.

## 4. Outreach Ask Templates by Source Type

### 4.1 Platform ask

Use when contacting `The Herd`, `On The Box`, `StockLive`, `Cattlesales`, `FarmGate`, or similar:

1. Briefly describe Signal Scout as a buyer-side discovery and alerting tool.
2. Make clear that Signal Scout does not aim to replicate their marketplace.
3. State that users would still go back to the source platform to bid, enquire, or transact.
4. Ask for permission to ingest factual listing metadata only.
5. Offer to attribute and deep-link every alert back to the original listing.
6. Ask whether they prefer:
   - permission for public-page ingestion;
   - an API/feed;
   - a partnership or referral arrangement.

### 4.2 Agent ask

Use when contacting `Elders`, `Nutrien`, `Ian Weir`, `Donovan`, `Farrell McCrohon`, or regional independents:

1. Describe Signal Scout as a buyer-matching tool for serious cattle buyers.
2. Ask whether they are comfortable with Signal Scout ingesting:
   - public sale calendars;
   - sale-entry PDFs;
   - subscribed email alerts;
   - facts-only listing metadata.
3. Emphasise that Signal Scout would link buyers back to the agent/source.
4. Offer an opt-out and source-by-source controls.
5. Ask whether they would rather provide a regular PDF or email feed directly.

## 6. Pre-Send Checklist

Before sending outreach for any source:

1. Confirm the legal posture in the source register.
2. Confirm the current brand and contact details.
3. Decide the exact ask:
   - scrape permission;
   - email ingestion permission;
   - PDF ingestion permission;
   - API / feed access;
   - referral / partnership conversation.
4. Prepare one-paragraph explanation of Signal Scout.
5. Prepare one-paragraph explanation of how source attribution and buyer redirection will work.
6. Log the outreach date and target in the source register once sent.

## 7. Suggested Internal Decisions Before Outreach

Before contacting anyone, Drumquil Signal should settle these internal positions:

1. Whether Signal Scout will ever show more than facts + link-back.
2. Whether it will store source photos.
3. Whether it wants a referral/lead model or only a data-permission model.
4. Which sources are essential enough to justify a commercial agreement.
5. Which mailbox/domain will be used for authorised alert subscriptions.
